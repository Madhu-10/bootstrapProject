WEDDING PHOTOGRAPHY

Unicorn Photography, we have been specializing in wedding photography for over 5 years, delivering exceptional services to clients across the country. Our team is dedicated to capturing every moment of your special day with creative and stunning images that will be cherished for years to come.

In addition to our photography services, we also offer professional video editing services, which include multi-lingual tracks, to create a beautiful and unforgettable wedding video. We also provide the option of Live Webcast, so your family and friends can be a part of your special day even if they can’t attend in person.

At Unicorn Photography, we believe that wedding photography is an art form that requires a skilled eye and a deep appreciation for the beauty and emotion of the occasion. Our team of experienced photographers is dedicated to creating timeless images that will tell the story of your wedding day for years to come.

At Unicorn Photography, we pride ourselves on our exceptional customer service. From your initial consultation to the delivery of your final images, we will work closely with you to ensure that your wedding photography experience is seamless and stress-free.
